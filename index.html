IEEE PES

IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES), a key technical society under IEEE at S.B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management, and Research, Nagpur, is a global hub for professionals, researchers, and students in electric power and energy. Focused on power systems engineering and renewable energy integration, it contributes to advancements in grid stability and smart grid technologies.

IEEE SB

An IEEE Student Branch gives students a community of peers, and a connection to faculty and industry professionals who drive innovation in countless technical fields. Student involvement in Branch activities, whether special projects, social and technical meetings, outreach programs, conferences, local Section or Regional opportunities, etc. can help develop a record of accomplishment and capabilities beyond the norm.
More About SAE >

ARYVARTA

ARYAVARTA ROBOTICS is the robotics arm of the SAE Collegiate Club at S.B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management, and Research, Nagpur. As part of the overarching Society of Automotive Engineers framework, this specialized club is dedicated to advancing robotics exploration. Members actively engage in diverse hands-on projects, spanning industrial automation, autonomous systems, and mechatronics.

The Elite Racers

The Elite Racers team at SB Jain College, under the umbrella of the SAE Collegiate Club, is a dynamic group of students passionate about pushing the boundaries of automotive engineering. Specializing in Formula SAE (FSAE), the team undertakes the exhilarating challenge of conceptualizing, constructing, and racing high-performance, formula-style cars. This hands-on initiative not only allows members to apply theoretical engineering concepts but also cultivates invaluable skills in teamwork, innovation, and problem-solving. The Elite Racers regularly participate in FSAE competitions, providing a platform to showcase their cutting-edge designs and compete with other universities on a national and international level.

More About IEEE PES

IEEE PES

IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES), a key technical society under IEEE at S.B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management, and Research, Nagpur, is a global hub for professionals, researchers, and students in electric power and energy. Focused on power systems engineering and renewable energy integration, it contributes to advancements in grid stability and smart grid technologies. IEEE PES facilitates knowledge exchange through conferences, publications, and industry standards development. It also plays a vital role in professional development, keeping members updated on the latest trends in the dynamic landscape of power and energy. The society's commitment extends to educational initiatives, fostering understanding and enthusiasm for power and energy concepts.
More About SAE ARYAVARTA

ARYVARTA

ARYAVARTA ROBOTICS is the robotics arm of the SAE Collegiate Club at S.B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management, and Research, Nagpur. As part of the overarching Society of Automotive Engineers framework, this specialized club is dedicated to advancing robotics exploration. Members actively engage in diverse hands-on projects, spanning industrial automation, autonomous systems, and mechatronics. The club's collaborative initiatives include designing robotic systems, integrating cutting-edge sensors and actuators, and programming sophisticated control algorithms. This multidisciplinary approach seamlessly combines aspects of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, and computer science, driving innovation in the field of robotics.
